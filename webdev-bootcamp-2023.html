Bad Website Club!

Free Web Development Bootcamp

Jess and Carmen have been running free bootcamps for a few years, and we're happy to be offering these through the Bad Website Club now!

Details

  • No Signups, no mandatory attendance, no tests: This is completely free and paced as you like!
  • Materials and free certificate are to be found on freeCodeCamp's Responsive Web Design certification
  • You can ask questions and collaborate on the freeCodeCamp forum
  • You can chat and collaborate on the Bad Website Club Discord Channel
